About STAYFINDR

STAYFINDR is your ultimate European hotel search companion, connecting you directly to real-time hotel prices from Booking.com across 29 major European destinations. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway in Paris, a business trip to London, or a family vacation in Barcelona, we make finding the perfect accommodation effortless.

Our platform specializes in European travel, offering localized search results with native language booking pages. From budget-friendly hostels to luxury Junior Suites, we help you discover accommodations that match your style and budget.

Why Choose STAYFINDR?

Unlike generic hotel search engines, STAYFINDR focuses exclusively on Europe, providing:

  • Real-time Booking.com integration - Live prices and availability
  • Localized experience - Spanish bookings for Palma, French for Paris, etc.
  • Smart room filtering - Including exclusive Junior Suite options
  • Mobile-optimized design - Perfect for travel planning on-the-go
  • No hidden fees - Transparent pricing directly from hotels
